Roaring '20s New Years Eve

I love throwing parties so I always felt like I would thrive in the world of Gatsby and the Roaring 20's.

It turns out the 2020's are an absolute dumpster fire I wouldn't wish on my worst enemy, but before covid ravaged everything, we managed to sneak in one more epic, world-changing, love-producing party.

So I don't believe we caused covid with this party, but over 300 people showed up and 3 people did vomit in our office where we hosted it and the landlord was very mad. So we could've made better choices.
